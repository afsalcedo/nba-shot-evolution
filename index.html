Glossary
FG%: Field goal percentage (made ÷ attempts)
3P%: 3-point field goal percentage
3PA Rate: Share of shots that are 3-point attempts
eFG%: Effective FG% = (FGM + 0.5 × 3PM) ÷ FGA
ΔFG%: FG% minus league average for that season
